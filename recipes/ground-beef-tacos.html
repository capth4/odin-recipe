Ground Beef Tacos

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Canola Oil
  • Shredded Cheese
  • 1/2 Lettuce
  • 2 Tomato
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Cook the ground beef on skillet until brown.
  2. When brown, add the taco seasoning and a little water. Mix it and simmer on low for another 5 minutes.
  3. Chop the tomato, onion, and cilantro, and mix in a bowl. Put to the side.
  4. Chop the lettuce and put to the side.
  5. Start heating up another skillet with canola oil on medium heat.
  6. Put corn tortilla on the skillet and put some ground beef on the tortilla. Fold it.
  7. Cook on each side for about 1 minute, by flipping it over, until nice and crunchy.
  8. Build your tacos and enjoy!
